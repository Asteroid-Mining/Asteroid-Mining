Digger-Class Drone

The brute force of the swarm. Diggers are heavily armored, slow-moving units equipped with anchoring harpoons and micro-plasma cutters designed to shatter solid rock.

Plasma Array Primary Tool
Heavy Armor Class
Micro-Harpoons Anchoring System

Zero-G Excavation

Because drilling pushes a spacecraft backward in zero gravity, Diggers must first fire explosive micro-harpoons into the rock to anchor themselves securely to the surface.

Once tethered, they deploy plasma cutters and percussive chisels to break the ore into manageable chunks. Diggers do not carry the ore; they simply break it loose and leave it floating in a localized tethered net for the Hauler class to collect.

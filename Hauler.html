Hauler-Class Drone

The logistics backbone of the operation. Haulers are essentially giant engines attached to magnetic cargo nets, designed to move massive quantities of raw ore from the dig site to the Mothership.

High-Yield Thruster Array
Electro-Net Cargo System
5,000 kg Max Tow Capacity

Cooperative Towing

Haulers arrive at the coordinates left by the Diggers. They use electromagnetic nets to scoop up the loose ore and engage their high-efficiency ion thrusters to ferry the material back to orbit.

If a particularly massive chunk of high-grade platinum is excavated, a single Hauler may not have enough thrust to move it. Through the swarm's mesh network, a Hauler can call for backup, allowing five or six Haulers to magnetically latch onto the same boulder and tow it back to the Queen cooperatively.

Recycler-Class Drone

Mining asteroids is dangerous work. When a drone suffers catastrophic damage from micro-meteoroids or sudden rock fractures, the Recycler class is dispatched to salvage the remains.

Salvage Primary Directive
Grapple Arms Primary Tool
High Expendability

The Expendability Protocol

The primary advantage of swarm robotics is redundancy. The Mothership does not waste energy mourning or attempting complex repairs on heavily damaged units like Digger-73. Instead, it activates the Expendability Protocol.

Recycler drones are dispatched to the coordinates of the downed unit. Using precise cutting lasers and grappling arms, they dismantle the broken drone, separating the valuable onboard processors, unspent propellant, and refined metals from the useless scrap. This salvaged material is then fed back into the Mothership's 3D printers to manufacture a brand-new replacement unit.
